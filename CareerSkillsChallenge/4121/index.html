Welcome to Central Valley Pets

Central Valley Pets Inc. is a compassionate and community-driven adoption center dedicated to finding loving homes for animals in need. Located in the heart of Central Valley, this non-profit organization rescues and rehabilitates cats, dogs, and small animals, ensuring they receive proper medical care, training, and socialization before adoption. With a warm and welcoming environment, the center offers spacious play areas, cozy kennels, and a dedicated team of caregivers who provide personalized attention to each pet. Central Valley Pets Inc. works closely with local veterinarians and volunteers to promote responsible pet ownership, offering educational programs and adoption counseling. Whether you're looking for a playful puppy, a cuddly kitten, or a loyal senior pet, this adoption center is committed to matching every animal with the perfect family.
